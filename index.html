Privacy Policy

App Name: AMANKASIR (Point of Sales)

Developer: AmanStudio

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AMANKASIR. We respect your privacy and are deeply committed to protecting your business operations. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information. By installing and using AMANKASIR, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Offline-First Architecture & Data Security

AMANKASIR is designed as a strict offline-first application. Your business data is your asset. Therefore:

  • No Cloud Syncing: All transaction records, customer data, product catalogs, inventory logs, and financial reports are stored locally on your device's internal storage using an embedded database.
  • No Data Collection: We do not transmit, upload, sell, or collect any of your operational data, transaction history, or customer information to our servers or any third-party services.

3. Device Permissions Explained

To function as a Point of Sales system, AMANKASIR requires specific device permissions. We only use these permissions for local operations:

  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA): Required exclusively for the barcode scanner feature to identify products. Images are processed locally in real-time and are never recorded, saved, or transmitted.
  • Bluetooth (android.permission.BLUETOOTH / BLUETOOTH_CONNECT): Required exclusively to pair, connect, and print transaction receipts via external Bluetooth thermal printers.
  • Storage (android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Required to export financial reports (PDF/Excel/CSV) to your local file manager and to create local database backups upon your request.
  • Internet (android.permission.INTERNET): Required only during the initial setup to verify your software license key (Serial Number) and check for application updates. It is not used to transmit user data.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application does not integrate with third-party tracking, advertising, or analytics SDKs that silently harvest user data.
